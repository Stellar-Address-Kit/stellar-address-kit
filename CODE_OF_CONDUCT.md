# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of level of experience, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards
- **Be Respectful**: Be kind to others. Do not insult or put down other contributors.
- **Inclusive Language**: Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- **Graceful Criticism**: Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- **Harassment-Free**: We have zero tolerance for harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at **lewechigodsfavour@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Maintainers are expected to take appropriate corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.
